What is Data Analysis?

It is the science that seeks to examine a data set to better understand the information it contains so that people can make informed decisions. Data analysis consists of submitting the data to a series of operations, to obtain precise conclusions that help us achieve our objectives. These operations can consist of transformations, debugging, cleaning and the correct storage of the optimized data. Currently, many industries use data analysis to draw conclusions and decide actions to implement.
